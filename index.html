Our Story

About Keepsy

Supplying since 3 years as a trusted, Made in India brand, to major retailers and brands including Archies & Chumbak. We are completely automated — India's up-and-coming largest AI wholesaler.

Crafting Excellence Since Day One

At Keepsy, we believe that every product should bring joy and create lasting memories. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide retailers across India with the finest quality toys, gifts, and décor items.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality while ensuring that our products are safe, sustainable, and crafted with love. Every item in our collection is carefully selected and tested to meet our rigorous quality standards.

Our partnerships with major brands like Archies and Chumbak reflect our commitment to excellence and our ability to deliver products that meet the highest industry standards.
